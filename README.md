## Valve Ranking System (VRS) — Additional Information

This section constitutes the **Additional Information** required under Valve Tournament Operation Requirements.

- **VRS Subdivision Used:**  
  - South America

- **VRS Filters Applied:**  
  - Rosters must have a plurality of Brazilian citizenships among participating players  
  - Rosters ranked within the top twelve (12) positions of the Global VRS are excluded  

- **VRS Starting Rank:**  
  - Highest-ranked eligible Roster within the South America VRS subdivision

- **Number of Direct VRS Invites:**  
  - 8 Rosters

- **Invite Date:**  
  - 16 March 2026

- **VRS Publication Used:**  
  - 02 March 2026 (for invitations and initial seeding)

- **Visa Region:**  
  - Brazil

- **Integrity Disqualification Rules:**  
  - Rosters with active VAC bans  
  - Rosters violating Gamers Club Anti-Cheat policies  
  - Rosters failing eligibility requirements as defined in the official rulebook  

Invitations are issued sequentially according to the VRS order.  
Invited Rosters have seventy-two (72) business hours to accept or decline the invitation.  
If a Roster declines or fails to respond, the invitation is passed to the next eligible Roster until all eight (8) slots are filled.

The final list of invited Rosters will be published publicly and will not be altered except as required to maintain compliance with Valve Tournament Operation Requirements.

## Open Qualifiers

- **Number of Qualifiers:** 4  
- **Slots per Qualifier:** 2  
- **Total Qualification Slots:** 8  

**Format**
- Maximum of 512 teams per qualifier
- Single Elimination bracket
- Early rounds: Best-of-1
- Qualification matches: Best-of-3

---

## Swiss Stage

- **Teams:** 16  
  - 8 Direct VRS Invites  
  - 8 Open Qualifier Teams  

**Format**
- Swiss System
- 5 rounds
- Buchholz pairing system
- Advancement at 3 wins
- Elimination at 3 losses
- No team may face the same opponent more than once

**Match Formats**
- Opening rounds: Best-of-1  
- Advancement and elimination matches: Best-of-3  

---

## Playoffs

- **Teams:** 8  
- **Bracket:** Single Elimination  
- **Match Format:** Best-of-3  

---

## Offline Grand Final

- **Teams:** 2  
- **Match Format:** Best-of-5  
- **Environment:** LAN  
- **Location:** State of São Paulo, Brazil  

Tournament PCs, monitors, noise-cancelling headsets and LAN infrastructure are provided by the organiser.  
Players may bring personal mice, keyboards, in-ears and mousepads.  
External communication software is prohibited.

## Competitive Integrity

- All matches are played using Gamers Club Anti-Cheat.
